## Highlight 
- Prof. Younghyun Kim delivered an invited talk on "Integrated Photonics: From LIDAR Chip to CPO/LPO" in the workshop session.
- Seokhyun Yoon, Jihyeon Lee, and Seonghyeok Bae presented oral presentations.
- Kyungjin Cho, Seokyeong Shin, Taewon Jin, and Daehong Kim presented poster presentations.
