## Highlight 
- Prof. Younghyun Kim delivered an invited talk on "Integrated Photonics: From LIDAR Chip to CPO/LPO" in the workshop session.
- Seokhyun Yoon, Jihyeon Lee, and Seonghyeok Bae gave oral presentations.
- Kyungjin Cho, Seokyeong Shin, and Taewon Jin presented poster sessions.
