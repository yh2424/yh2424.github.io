## Highlight
- ASDL Master's students Seokyoun Shin and Seokhyeon Yoon received the **Best Paper Award** for their research at the **2025 International Symposium on Microelectronics and Packaging**.

- Seokyoung Shin's research topic: **Design of Low-Loss SiN Stitching Structures for Wafer-Level Optical Interconnects**
- Seokhyeon Yoon's research topic: **Design of a Low-Loss SiN Evanescent Coupler for Co-Packaged Optics**
