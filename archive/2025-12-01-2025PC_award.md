## Highlight
- ASDL PI, Professor Younghyun Kim, received the **Contribution Award** at **Photonics Conference 2025.**
- ASDL Master's students Jonggeon Lee received the **Best Oral Presentation Award** for their research at **Photonics Conference 2025**.
- Jonggeon Lee's research topic: **Comprehensive Design Optimization and Eye Diagram Simulation for 400-Gbps PAM6 Micro Ring Modulator**
